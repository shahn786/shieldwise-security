Your Hotel Security page does not look or feel like your Commercial Security and Hospital Security pages because:

You are missing the shared critical CSS style block in the <head> that sets the main design (fonts, colors, spacing).

The “above-the-fold” hero section does not use the same CSS rules as your Commercial Security page.

You’re not inlining any critical styles for speed/consistency—you only link external CSS.

Font and color palette, background, and preloads are missing.

Minor: your Font Awesome version and meta includes could be mismatched (less likely, but double-check your partials).

How to Fix:
Add the same inlined <style> block (and associated preloads) from your Commercial Security page into your Hotel Security page’s <head>.
