Your Font Awesome icons are not showing because your Content Security Policy (CSP) does not allow stylesheets or fonts from the Font Awesome CDN. Even though you included the Font Awesome stylesheet and preloaded fonts, your CSP is blocking them.

The Problem
Your CSP for font-src is:

html
Copy
Edit
font-src 'self' https://fonts.gstatic.com https://cdn.jsdelivr.net;
But your Font Awesome stylesheet and its fonts are served from cdnjs.cloudflare.com, specifically:

CSS: https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/font-awesome/6.5.1/css/all.min.css

Fonts: https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/font-awesome/6.5.1/webfonts/...

So, the browser blocks Font Awesome fonts (woff2 files) because cdnjs.cloudflare.com isn’t in your font-src list.

How to Fix
1. Update Your CSP to Allow cdnjs.cloudflare.com
Find this section in your <meta http-equiv="Content-Security-Policy"...> tag:

html
Copy
Edit
font-src 'self' https://fonts.gstatic.com https://cdn.jsdelivr.net;
Change it to:

html
Copy
Edit
font-src 'self' https://fonts.gstatic.com https://cdn.jsdelivr.net https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com;
2. (Optional but Recommended) Allow Font Awesome’s CSS as Well
Your style-src should already allow https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com, which is correct:

html
Copy
Edit
style-src 'self' 'nonce-security-style' 'unsafe-inline' https://fonts.googleapis.com https://cdn.jsdelivr.net https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com;
So you only need to add the font domain!

3. Clear Your Cache and Refresh
After updating your CSP, clear your browser cache and do a hard refresh (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R).

Example: Corrected CSP Meta Tag
html
Copy
Edit
<meta http-equiv="Content-Security-Policy"
  content="
    default-src 'self';
    script-src 'self' 'nonce-security-script' https://www.googletagmanager.com https://code.jquery.com https://cdn.jsdelivr.net https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com https://www.google-analytics.com;
    style-src 'self' 'nonce-security-style' 'unsafe-inline' https://fonts.googleapis.com https://cdn.jsdelivr.net https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com;
    font-src 'self' https://fonts.gstatic.com https://cdn.jsdelivr.net **https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com**;
    img-src 'self' data: https: *.google-analytics.com;
    connect-src 'self' https://www.google-analytics.com;
    frame-src 'self' https://www.google.com;
    object-src 'none';
    base-uri 'self';
    form-action 'self';
    upgrade-insecure-requests;
  ">
(see the bolded new addition)

TL;DR
Add https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com to your font-src in your Content Security Policy.

Save, clear cache, and reload.

Your icons should now display perfectly!
