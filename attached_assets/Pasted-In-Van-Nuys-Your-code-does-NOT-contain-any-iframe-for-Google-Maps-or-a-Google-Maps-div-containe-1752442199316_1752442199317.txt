2. Why does the FAQ accordion not expand in Van Nuys but works in Burbank?
Common Reason:
The FAQ “accordion” uses Bootstrap Collapse JS.

This requires Bootstrap JS and jQuery to be loaded and not blocked/errored.

The FAQ markup in Burbank is standard Bootstrap 4 accordion markup.

If the scripts are missing, deferred in the wrong order, or error out, your FAQ won’t expand/collapse.

What to Check:
In Burbank (WORKS):
html
Copy
Edit
<script src="https://code.jquery.com/jquery-3.5.1.slim.min.js" defer></script>
<script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/popper.js@1.16.1/dist/umd/popper.min.js" defer></script>
<script src="https://stackpath.bootstrapcdn.com/bootstrap/4.5.2/js/bootstrap.min.js" defer></script>
In Van Nuys (LIKELY PROBLEM):
You might be missing one or more of these scripts, or they might load in the wrong order, or you use <script defer> in a way that causes Bootstrap JS to run before jQuery is ready.

You may also be missing the FAQ markup entirely in the Van Nuys file.

How to Fix the FAQ Accordion:

Remove defer if it causes race conditions; try without it to test.
