Inline critical CSS for hero/top fold; defer the rest:

Remove the hidden <img>; instead, set the hero background-image and provide a real, visible <img> (or a <picture>) in the DOM inside the hero for crawlability, but not duplicated. Alternatively add role="img" + proper aria-label to the hero if you keep it as CSS background.
