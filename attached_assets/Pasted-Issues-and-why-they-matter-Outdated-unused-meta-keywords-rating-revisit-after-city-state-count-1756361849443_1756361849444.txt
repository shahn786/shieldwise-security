Issues (and why they matter)

Outdated/unused meta
keywords, rating, revisit-after, city/state/country/zipcode/coordinates/region/locality, ICBM offer no ranking value and add noise.

Duplicate/contradictory metadata
Two author tags; HTML root uses itemtype="LocalBusiness" but your primary JSON-LD centers on SecurityService—pick one primary entity and reference the others via @id/@graph.

OG type
og:type="business.business" is rarely used and under-documented; use website (or article if it’s content-heavy) to maximize compatibility.

Speakable
SpeakableSpecification has limited support and mainly for news; safe to remove unless you’re targeting eligible surfaces.

Schema offer pricing
price: "45-85" and UnitPriceSpecification.price: "45-85" are invalid (must be numbers). Use priceSpecification with minPrice/maxPrice or surface only priceRange at the business level.

Review/aggregateRating policy risk
If the reviews/ratings aren’t verifiable and on your page, Google may ignore the markup. Avoid synthetic reviewer names/titles; ensure reviews are visible on page.

Multiple H1s
You have an H1 in the hero and another later. It’s valid HTML5, but for clarity and snippet generation, prefer one primary H1.

Fonts/CSS performance

You preload Google Fonts as as="style"—good—but also include the same stylesheet later. Ensure no duplicate fetch and add display=swap.

Font Awesome + Bootstrap 4.5.2 are heavy; consider self-hosting or deferring non-critical CSS.

Accessibility/INP
100vh hero + heavy animations can hurt LCP/INP on mobile; ensure the hero image is preloaded and sized with explicit width/height, and keep initial JS light.

NAP consistency
The phone number uses a placeholder (510) 555-0123. If your GMB/GBP lists a different number, consistency matters.
