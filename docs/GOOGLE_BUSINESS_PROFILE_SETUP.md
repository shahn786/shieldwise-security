## Overview

Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is **critical** for local SEO. It makes your business appear in:
- Google Maps searches
- Local Pack results (top 3 businesses)
- Knowledge Panel (right side of search results)
- "Near me" searches on mobile

For a security company serving 182 California cities, a properly optimized profile can drive significant local traffic.

### Step 4: Verify Your Business

Google requires verification before you can manage your profile.

**Verification Methods:**
1. **Postcard (Most Common):**
   - Google mails a postcard to your business address with verification code
   - Takes 5-14 days to arrive
   - Enter code in Google Business Profile dashboard

2. **Phone Call:**
   - Available for some businesses
   - Google calls your business phone number with verification code

3. **Email:**
   - Available if your domain is verified in Google Search Console
   - Instant verification

4. **Instant Verification (If Available):**
   - If you're already verified in Google Search Console for the same domain
   - May be offered automatically

**Recommended:** Use email or instant verification if available (fastest).

## Part 4: Collect Reviews

### Why Reviews Matter

- **Local Ranking Factor:** Reviews are one of the top 3 local SEO ranking factors
- **Trust Signal:** 88% of consumers trust online reviews
- **Conversion Driver:** More reviews = more customers

**Target:** 50+ reviews within first 6 months

**3. In-Person Request:**
- After completing a successful event or contract

**4. Incentivize Ethically:**
- ‚ùå Don't offer payment for reviews (against Google policies)

### Getting Your Review Link

1. Open Google Business Profile dashboard
2. Go to **"Home"**
3. Click **"Get more reviews"**
4. Copy the short URL (e.g., `https://g.page/r/XXXXXXXXXX/review`)
5. Use this link in emails, texts, and business cards

## Part 7: Advanced Optimization

### Local Pack Ranking Factors

**Top 3 Factors:**
1. **Relevance:** Category, description, services match search query
2. **Distance:** Proximity to searcher
3. **Prominence:** Reviews, backlinks, citations, website authority

**How to Improve Each:**

**Relevance:**
- Use exact keywords in business description
- Complete all service listings
- Answer all Q&A with keyword-rich responses

**Distance:**
- Expand service areas to cover more cities
- Create location-specific content on website
- Build citations in each service area

**Prominence:**
- Get 50+ reviews (5-star average)
- Build local backlinks (Chamber, BBB, industry directories)
- Increase NAP citations across the web
- Weekly posts and engagement
